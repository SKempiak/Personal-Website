Hello, welcome to personal webpage! My name is Samson and I am a senior at 
Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute. I am currently studying Computer Science and 
Electrical Engineering. I currently currently doing research at RPI's Wireless 
AI and Systems (WAYS) Lab. Primarily I work to develop systems to measure network
analytics, and use that data to determine ways to design and implement algorithms
for mobile networks.

I am involved in clubs like the Rensselaer Spaceflight Society, RPI weighlifting,
and Union Show Techs. I have learned a wide variety of skills from wireless
communication protocols for embedded applications to setting up scenes with the 
lighting systems we have available.

I am a big advocate for student government and its transparency. I have been on
the Rensselaer Union's Executive board since coming to RPI, and this previous year
I decided to take on a greater role. I performed as the Union Annual Report
committee chair. In this position, I took the lead on the 60 page document that 
thoroughly documented the usage of the schools budget, which was around 5 million
dollars for Financial Year 2027.
